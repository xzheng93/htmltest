Human Movement Science, Machine Learning, Wearable Sensors

My name is Xiaoping Zheng, a Ph.D. student at the department of Human movement sciences at University of Groningen. My supervisors are Prof. Dr. Claudine Lamoth, Prof. Dr. Michiel Reneman and Prof. Dr. Bert Otten.
  My research focuses on the intersection of Human movement sciences, AI, and wearable sensors. I am particularly interested in employing machine learning methods to analyze the movement characteristics of patients with Low Back Pain during daily life environments based on wearable sensor data.
  I obtained the B.E degree and the M.E degree from China University of Geosciences (Wuhan).
